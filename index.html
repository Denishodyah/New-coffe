About our coffee break

Break & Coffee coffee house is a cozy refuge in the heart of the city, where every visitor finds his oasis of calm and inspiration. Upon entering this mesmerizing world of coffee aromas you plunge into the atmosphere of warmth and hospitality. The walls are decorated with travel photos and drawings by local artists, creating an atmosphere of travel and adventure.
